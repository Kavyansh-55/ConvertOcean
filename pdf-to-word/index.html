Frequently Asked Questions

      

        

          

            Do files get uploaded to a server?
            
          

          

            No uploads are performed. All data parsing, calculations, and rendering occur entirely in your local browser sandbox memory, and files are automatically removed when you close the tab.
          

        

        

          

            Does it work offline?
            
          

          

            Yes. Once you load this tool page, you can disconnect from the internet and convert files. The conversion engine runs entirely client-side.
          

        

        

          

            Does it preserve complex Word formatting?
            
          

          

            The tool extracts text nodes and formats them into paragraph elements within a Microsoft Word compatible HTML structure. While simple styling and layouts are fully retained, highly complex desktop-published PDF grids may require manual alignment inside Word.
