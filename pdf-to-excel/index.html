Frequently Asked Questions

      

        

          

            Do files get uploaded to a server?
            
          

          

            No uploads are performed. All data parsing, calculations, and rendering occur entirely in your local browser sandbox memory, and files are automatically removed when you close the tab.
          

        

        

          

            Does it work offline?
            
          

          

            Yes. Once you load this tool page, you can disconnect from the internet and convert files. The conversion engine loads pdf.js and SheetJS locally.
          

        

        

          

            How does the parser reconstruct cell tables?
            
          

          

            The script detects text positioning coordinate parameters (x, y coordinate points) inside the PDF. Elements lying on the same horizontal row (within a small pixel tolerance margin) are grouped together as columns, preserving the general grid alignment.
