Privacy Policy.

Your privacy is our core guarantee. ConvertOcean operates under a strict offline-first, client-side execution model to ensure your data stays yours.

1. 100% Client-Side File Processing

ConvertOcean converts, compresses, splits, and compiles your files entirely in your browser using JavaScript and WebAssembly compiled engines. Your files are **never uploaded to, processed on, or stored on any external servers or databases**. All processing is executed by your device's local CPU/GPU inside sandboxed browser tab memory.

2. Zero Telemetry & Data Collection

We believe in complete user anonymity. We do not track, collect, or record any details regarding:

  • Your files (file contents, filenames, sizes, extensions, or metadata).
  • Your identity (we do not require accounts, signups, email verification, or payment methods).
  • Your conversion history or utility usage statistics.

3. Cookie Policy

ConvertOcean does not use tracking cookies, targeting advertising trackers, or profiling algorithms. We utilize local browser storage (`localStorage`) solely to store UI preferences, such as your dark mode settings (`light` / `dark`), ensuring a seamless experience when you return.

4. External Libraries & CDN Integrity

Certain pages fetch script files (such as SheetJS for Excel spreadsheets or html2pdf for PDF compilations) via public CDN services. These script assets are loaded by your browser solely to run the programs locally. CDNs do not receive or inspect your file data, which is parsed only after the script is loaded into your local browser window.

5. Children's Privacy

Since ConvertOcean does not collect any personal data, our platform is completely safe for children and students to use for educational work and document processing without any risk of metadata profiling.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. However, our core commitment—that your files are never uploaded to any remote server and processing remains entirely client-side—is non-negotiable and will never change.
